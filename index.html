We welcome you to 'The Bear'
In the era of digital communication, where news and updates fly across screens at the speed of light, we students who aim for a better future have to adapt and run twice as fast. Welcome to The Bear: our own school newspaper
BY JÚLIA TORQUES

AUGUST 7, 2023

Fostering Connection: Building a Unified Community
In an age when technology accustomed us to living at 220 volts, the school newspaper is a bridge between students, teachers and parents that aims to stop you from this day-to-day rush and enjoy some good content. It serves as a means to highlight our experiences, achievements and shared aspirations at Maple Bear Barra da Tijuca. By showcasing the diverse talents, accomplishments and stories of our school members, the newspaper becomes a testament to our unity, updating readers on the latest news inside and outside the school. This connection that The Bear tries to build is one that promotes a sense of belonging, school pride and the cultivation of strong relationships.
Empowering Information: Knowledge at Your Fingertips
The school newspaper is not just a collection of articles; it is a shared knowledge, insights and updates that keeps us informed on school affairs and will later become a relic for the next generation of students. Whether it's highlighting upcoming events, interviewing faculty and students or updating the weekly menu, the newspaper makes sure everyone is in the know. It encourages critical thinking by presenting a diverse range of perspectives on various subjects, inspiring students to engage in meaningful conversations inside and outside the classroom.
Igniting Inspiration: Amplifying Voices and Ideas
Behind every newspaper article is a student eager to share stories. The journal serves as a launch pad for creativity, allowing budding writers, photographers and designers to hone their skills and amplify their voices. By showcasing these talents, the newspaper sparks inspiration and encourages others to explore their own creative potential. The newspaper becomes a catalyst for positive change and innovation. In conclusion, through the school newspaper, we embark on a collective journey of growth, learning, and shared experience. The Bear is more than words in a website; it's a living embodiment of our community's spirit, a space where knowledge and creativity walk together. Let’s become a bear in our community.
